About CFE

CFE is the first center for a technologic cooperation with a Chamber of Industry and Trade. Our mission is to reinforce the cooperation between the high technology channels in France and in Russia through targeted operations allowing the companies of all sizes to identify opportunities for technological, industrial and business cooperations.

Our experts have a double proficiency in leading edge technological sectors in France and Russia. We are bringing our knowledge of the markets to the table, our privileged relationships in State organizations, as well as our know-how for the promotion and representation of your business. We are part of the France-Russia Industrial Trade Chamber which has been supporting the economic and trade bilateral relations for the past 20 years.
