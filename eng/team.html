CFE’s team: Export professionals

CFE gathers a professional team of experts specialized in business support in Russia, Central Asia and the Caucasus. It benefits from the expertise and experience of CIFAL.

CIFAL is the oldest and largest French international business and service firm dedicated to Russia and the Central Asian/Caspian region. Since 1956, CIFAL has had dozens of industrial references in Russia, in the sectors of energy, infrastructure and high technologies.

We are well known by the French and Russian communities as well as by the French export system.

Each of CFE’s collaborators justifies of a couple of years’ experience working for CIFAL, in the French Embassy in Russia, at the trade department, for Ubifrance and BusinessFrance, always in the professions related to export.

Our collaborators have assisted hundreds of French companies with market research, finding the right distributors for their products, final clients or industrial partners in Russia, Central Asia and the Caucasus.
